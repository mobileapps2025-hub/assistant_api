# MCL Platform & Device Notes

This document covers platform-specific behaviour in the MCL Mobile App, distinguishing between iOS, Android, and Tablet where relevant.

---

## Department Navigation — iOS vs Android

### Jumping between departments in a checklist

- **iOS only**: Tap the **Magnifying Glass icon** (top left of the screen) to open a list of all departments in the checklist. Tap any department to jump directly to it.
  - Departments shown in **black** = all questions completed.
  - Departments shown in **red** = incomplete questions remain. The number of unanswered questions is shown in brackets.
- **Android**: Jumping between departments is **not supported**. This feature is available on iOS only. On Android, you must navigate through questions sequentially using the arrows or swipe gestures.

### Copying a checklist question

- **Android**: Long-press a question, then tap the **Copy** button that appears.
- **iOS**: Tap the question — it is copied automatically.

## Completing Tasks — Mobile vs Tablet

### On a mobile phone (swipe method)
1. Go to **Tasks Overview**.
2. Swipe the task from **right to left**.
3. Tap **Complete Task**.
> Note: The swipe method only works if the task does not require a photo or comment. If a photo or comment is required, you must use the Note method.

### On a mobile phone (Note method)
1. Open the task.
2. Tap the **Note Icon** (top right).
3. Set **Complete Task = Yes**.
4. Tap **Add**.

### On a tablet
1. Go to **Tasks Overview**.
2. Tap **Note** next to the task.
3. Tap the **Note Icon**.
4. Set **Complete Task = Yes**.
5. Tap **Add**.

---

## Checklist Progress — Device Binding

Checklist progress is **saved locally on the device**. It is not synced across devices. If you start a checklist on one device, you cannot continue it on another device.

---

## Department Order

- **In the MCL App**: The order of departments can be changed by **drag-and-drop** before starting the checklist.
- **In the Dashboard (Checklist Wizard)**: The department order cannot be changed from the Dashboard.

---

## Finishing a Checklist — Report Types
<!-- German: Wie beende ich eine Checkliste? / Checkliste abschließen / Bericht abschließen -->

After completing a checklist in the MCL App, tap **Finish Report** (German: **Bericht abschließen**) to upload the checklist. You will then choose how to share the report:

| Option | What it contains |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Full Report** | All checklist questions and their answers, including scores and any attached photos or comments. The complete inspection record. |
| **Results Only** | Only the final scores and results — no individual question details. Use this for a summary overview. |
| **Tasks Only** | Only the tasks that were created during the checklist inspection — no scores or question details. Use this to share action items. |

You can send the chosen report by entering an email address manually, selecting a stored address from your directory, or viewing it as a PDF on your device within the MCL app.
