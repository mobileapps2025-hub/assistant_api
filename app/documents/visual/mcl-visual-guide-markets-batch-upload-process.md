# Batch Upload Process for Markets

The batch upload feature allows you to process the entire market database in one go. By uploading a completed file, new markets are created, existing ones are updated, and markets not included in the upload are removed. This feature streamlines operations, ensuring consistency and saving time when managing extensive market data.
