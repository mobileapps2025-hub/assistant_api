# Managing App Synchronization

The **App Status** screen in the MCL app allows users to manage synchronization of locally stored data with the system. By selecting **Synchronize Now**, you can ensure that your reports and data are up-to-date and correctly synced. This screen also provides options to download PDF files attached to checklist questions, ensuring access to them even when offline.
