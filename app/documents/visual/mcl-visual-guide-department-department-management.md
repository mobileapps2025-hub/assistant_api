# Department Management

The Departments Overview screen is your starting point for managing department details such as creating, editing, or deleting records. To add a new department, simply click the "+" icon. This action opens the Department Editor where you enter the department’s name and description. Use the pencil and trash icons to edit or remove existing departments.
