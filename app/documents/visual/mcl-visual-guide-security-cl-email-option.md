# CL E-Mail Option

The CL E-Mail option manages the automated distribution of checklist reports. Users selected under CL E-Mail will receive all checklist reports for their assigned store, regardless of their participation in completing the checklist. These reports are sent as web reports and are available for download in PDF format. This feature functions like an automatic CC, ensuring roles like store managers, regional managers, and quality control have access to important reports.
