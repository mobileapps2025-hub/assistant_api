# Analytics Email Notification

Analytics emails are scheduled notifications that provide users with periodic performance summaries. Accessible under the **Analytics Email** screen (within the **Dashboard** under **Role Settings**), these emails contain details about best and worst scoring questions, performance trends, and aggregated results.
