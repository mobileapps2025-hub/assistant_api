# Selecting Roles in Checklist Wizard

In the Checklist Wizard, navigate to the 'Roles' tab where you can assign access to specific roles for a checklist. Check the boxes next to the roles you want to grant access, ensuring that only the selected roles will see the checklist in their MCL Apps. Once you've made your selections, click 'Continue' to proceed.
