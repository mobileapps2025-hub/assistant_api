# Adding Photos and Notes to a Task

In the MCL app, you can enhance tasks by attaching photos and notes for better context. Use the text box to enter a descriptive note; tap the area and begin typing. To convert speech to text, tap the microphone icon. Add visual context by tapping the camera icon to take or upload a photo.
