# Market List Interface

The Market List interface displays all the available markets you can access, such as 'Market 1' and 'Market 2'. Each market is represented by a row, and tapping on a market will open its checklist overview. This screen serves as the central starting point of the app, where users can select a market to begin working on checklists or reviewing prior activities.
