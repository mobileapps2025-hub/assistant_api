# App Color Management

The App Color Management section allows customization of the app's color scheme. Accessible from the Company Sub-Menu, this screen provides options to personalize the visual aspects of the interface, ensuring a consistent and attractive user experience.

App Color Management lets users customize the MCL App's visual style. In the color settings, you can change the main and secondary colors, as well as their corresponding font colors. You can also adjust the dark and light web colors and set the icons color to match your preference. Use the 'Restore' button to revert changes or 'Save' to apply your new settings.
