# Navigating the Photo Carousel

The Photo Carousel is the main area where you can view your photos. Use the left arrow to go back to the previous photo and the right arrow to proceed to the next one. This carousel cycles through all the filtered results, allowing you to review them in sequence. Clicking the photo itself has no additional functionality, such as zooming or opening in a popup.
