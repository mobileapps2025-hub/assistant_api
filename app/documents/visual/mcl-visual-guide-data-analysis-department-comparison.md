# Department Comparison

The Department Comparison tab offers insights into department performance across multiple markets. Users can specify attributes like company, department, and time period to generate a visual comparison in percentage form. This interactive diagram helps identify varying departmental effectiveness in different market contexts, facilitating focused decision-making.
