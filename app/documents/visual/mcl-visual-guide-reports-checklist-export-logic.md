# Checklist Export Logic

In the checklist export, values are represented by 0 and 1. A value of 0 indicates a negative response, such as not expected or not completed. A value of 1 indicates a positive response, such as expected or completed. Even checklists without a defined period are included in the export. These checklists are marked as not expected (0) but completed, ensuring transparency in checklist activities regardless of any scheduling criteria.
