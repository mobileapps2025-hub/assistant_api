# Delete a Question Permanently

To permanently delete a question in the MCL system, click the trash can icon next to the desired question. Confirm your decision when prompted. Be aware that this action cannot be undone, and the question will be removed from all checklists where it was previously included. If any checklist depended on this question, you need to edit it to remove or replace the missing question.
