# Company Profile Management

The company profile management section assists users in maintaining and configuring essential company data. Address fields allow entry of company address details, while the city/postal code fields capture location information. The phone field records the contact number, and the industry field logs the company's industry classification. You can configure the percentage display in reports using the toggle. The 'Active' indicator shows if the company is operational. Icons provide additional functions: e-mails configuration (gear icon) for address book setup and report distribution, sensor info (gear icon) for adding temperature sensors, and the edit (pencil icon) and delete (trash icon) for modifying or removing company information.
