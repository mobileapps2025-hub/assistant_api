# Viewing Tasks Overview

The tasks overview page in the MCL app allows you to see a list of upcoming tasks with their statuses. Tasks are displayed with the scheduled date, status such as 'Nicht gestartet' (Not started), and any attached notes or photos. You can navigate to this screen by selecting the 'calendar' icon at the bottom of the app interface.
