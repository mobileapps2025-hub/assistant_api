# Editing a Question

To edit an existing question, click the pencil icon next to it. You can modify the text, add additional information, change the question type, and set requirements for photos or comments. You can also adjust department assignments and define what constitutes a positive answer. After making changes, click 'Save' to update the question across all checklists. Changing department assignments will affect where the question appears in the Checklist Wizard.
