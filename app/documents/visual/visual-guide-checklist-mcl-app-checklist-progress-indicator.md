# Checklist Progress Indicator

The checklist screen features a progress indicator at the top, showing details about the checklist's current department and question progress. The center of the header displays the department name, indicating the department for which the checklist is being completed. Below the department name, the 'Departments [x / y]' indicator reveals progress through the departments, where 'x' represents the current department and 'y' the total number of departments.
