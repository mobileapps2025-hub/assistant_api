# Email Settings Panel

The Email panel in the Checklist Wizard allows you to specify recipients for automatic email reports. You can enter email addresses in the 'E-Mail' field and assign roles to define who receives notifications. Use the role management section to associate roles like Auditor, Company Administrator, and Team Member with the email settings.
