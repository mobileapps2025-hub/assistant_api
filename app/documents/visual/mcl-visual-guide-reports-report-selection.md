# Selecting a Reporting System

Users can choose between two types of reports: **Ad Hoc Reports** and **Canned Reports**. Selecting **Ad Hoc Reports** provides a PDF based on a single checklist execution. Opting for **Canned Reports** gives access to predefined standardized Excel reports.
