# Common Mistakes in Checklist Setup

When setting up a checklist, ensure that you select individual questions after choosing a department to avoid missing them. Remember that the checklist setup isn’t saved until you hit **Complete**. Roles and departments define visibility to stores and users. Use a semicolon (;) to separate email addresses instead of commas.
