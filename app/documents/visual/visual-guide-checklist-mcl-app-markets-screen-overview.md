# Markets Screen Overview

After logging into the MCL App, users are presented with the Markets screen. This screen allows users to access various markets such as 'Markt 1', 'Markt 2', and others, which are listed in a straightforward manner. Icons at the bottom, highlighted in a red box, provide shortcuts to complete checklists, view tasks, or navigate to other functions within the app.
