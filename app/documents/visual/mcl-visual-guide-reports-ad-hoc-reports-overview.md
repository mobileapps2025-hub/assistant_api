# Understanding Ad Hoc Reports

Ad Hoc reports are generated automatically every time a checklist is executed in the MCL App. These reports capture all questions, answers, tasks, photos, and comments, and are available as PDFs. They are stored in the dashboard for easy access, useful for operational analysis and compliance checks.
