# Device Push Notification (iOS)

The MCL app sends push notifications to the device's notification center on iOS devices. These notifications inform users about newly assigned tasks or tasks that are due today. Each notification includes the app's name, a brief message such as 'You have been assigned new tasks' or 'You have tasks due TODAY,' and a timestamp indicating when the notification was sent.
