# Question Selection in Checklist Wizard

To select specific questions for a department in the Checklist Wizard, navigate to the 'Questions' tab. Begin by choosing a department from the 'Departments' list on the left. Once a department is selected, the associated questions will appear. Manually check the boxes next to each question you wish to include. Note that selection is not automatic; each question must be individually selected.
