# Understanding the Bottom Menu Functions

The bottom menu of the app provides users with quick access to key functions. The Checklist Icon takes you to the store overview, where selecting a store opens its checklist. The Book Icon allows you to view checklist reports right on your device. With the Clock Icon, you can access the tasks overview, and the User Icon lets you adjust the app configuration.
