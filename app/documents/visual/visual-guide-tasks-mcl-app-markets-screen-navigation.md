# Markets Screen Navigation

Upon logging into the MCL App, users are taken to the Markets screen. Here, they can view a list of available markets such as 'Markt 1', 'Markt 2', and others. The navigation bar at the bottom, surrounded by a red box, allows users to access different functions or sections of the app quickly.
