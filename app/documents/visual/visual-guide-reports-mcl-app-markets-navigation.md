# Navigating the Markets Screen

Upon logging into the MCL App, users are taken to the Markets screen. Here, you can view a list of different markets such as 'Markt 1', 'Markt 2', and more. By utilizing the navigation icons highlighted in red at the bottom, users can easily complete tasks or access other features of the app.
