# Department Selection List

The department selection list provides options to assign tasks or responses to various departments. Departments like 'Gastonomie,' 'Personalhygiene,' and 'Eigentkontrollen' can be selected by checking the boxes next to their names. This feature helps in organizing tasks according to responsible departments.
