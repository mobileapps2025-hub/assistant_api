# Checklists Not Completed – Detail View

By clicking the 'Checklists Not Completed' KPI tile, you can access a detailed list of all checklists that haven't been completed for a selected reporting period. Each item in this list represents an individual missed checklist and includes details such as the checklist name, the market (store location), the recurrence schedule (e.g., daily, weekly), and the date range during which the checklist was expected to be completed. This detailed view helps provide an overview of which checklists were missed and when.
