# Notification Triggers and Rules

Push notifications are triggered by events like task creation or checklist completion, and are also time-based, adapting to summer or winter schedules. New task notifications appear at 08:30 or 14:30 during summer, and 09:30 or 15:30 in winter. Due task notifications are sent at 07:00 am in summer or 08:00 am in winter, with a maximum of three notifications per day. Notifications are influenced by user or market assignments.
