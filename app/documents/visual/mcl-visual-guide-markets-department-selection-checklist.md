# Department Selection for Checklists

In the MCL app, you can assign specific departments to a market by selecting them from a list using checkboxes. This ensures that only the chosen departments and their associated checklists appear in the app for that market. Make sure all departments your checklists require are selected, as unselected departments will not display their checklists. Even after importing markets via Batch Upload, departments must be assigned manually.
