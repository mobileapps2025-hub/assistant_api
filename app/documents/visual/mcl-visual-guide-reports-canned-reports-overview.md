# Canned Reports Overview

The Canned Reports module in the MCL app allows users to efficiently generate reports with various filtering options. Users can select the company, define a period (such as a day, week, month, quarter, or year), and choose specific dates within those periods. Additional options are available to filter the reports by checklist type. Once set, users can utilize the search bar to find specific reports and download them via the cloud icon. Exporting is supported in PDF format for further distribution or analysis.
