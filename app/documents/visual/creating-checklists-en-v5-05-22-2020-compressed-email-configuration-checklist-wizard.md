# Email Configuration in Checklist Wizard

In the Checklist Wizard, you can specify an email address for receiving reports generated from checklists. Navigate to the E-Mail tab, where you can enter a specific email address or select a role to assign report distribution. You can also choose entire departments to receive the reports, ensuring flexible configuration for targeted delivery.
