# Department Selection in Checklist Wizard

In the Checklist Wizard, use the 'Departments' panel to specify which departments the checklist will encompass. You can select multiple departments as needed. Keep in mind that selecting a department ensures the availability of the checklist for associated stores. However, it does not automatically include all questions related to that department in the checklist.
