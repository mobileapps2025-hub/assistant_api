# Managing Market List

The Markets page displays all configured stores, known as markets, in a list format. You can see details such as the market name, optional market ID, email, address, and assigned departments or tags. To add a new market, click the '+' icon to open the market creation form. You can edit an existing market by clicking the pencil icon next to the market entry, which opens the Market Configuration screen.
