# Accessing Checklist Reports

You can review your checklist reports directly on your mobile device without needing to access the web dashboard. Use the 'Date From' and 'Date To' selectors to specify the start and end dates for filtering reports. Tap the 'Search Button' to display the reports that match your criteria. Each report entry will show the market name, report ID, and the execution timestamp. To view more details, select a report entry and use the navigation arrow to open it.
