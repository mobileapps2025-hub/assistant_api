# Reports Overview Screen

The Reports Overview screen in the MCL app allows you to view checklist reports that have been executed on your device. These reports are available only during your current session and disappear when you log out. You can search for completed checklists by selecting a specific date range using the date fields and then tapping the 'Search' button.
