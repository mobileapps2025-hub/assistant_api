# Delete a Department

To permanently remove a department in the MCL Web app, click the trash can icon next to the desired department. Confirm the deletion when prompted. Be aware that questions linked to the department will not appear in the Checklist Wizard unless reassigned, and markets using this department will lose it. This action cannot be undone.
